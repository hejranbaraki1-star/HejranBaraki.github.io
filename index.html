Akbari Hejran

Computer Science Student

education & learning experience

My name is Akbari Hejran, and this is the story of my educational journey. I was born and raised in Afghanistan, where I completed my primary and secondary education. I studied at Sayed Noor Mohammad Shah Mina School in Afghanistan, where I spent many years learning the basics of science, mathematics, literature, and Islamic studies. My time at this school gave me a strong foundation and taught me the value of discipline, hard work, and persistence. I am grateful for the teachers who guided me and for the friends who supported me along the way.

After completing my school education in Afghanistan, I faced many challenges. Life in Afghanistan was not easy, and access to higher education was very limited. However, I never lost hope. I always dreamed of continuing my studies and building a better future for myself and my family. I worked hard, studied day and night, and kept my goals clear in my mind. I knew that education was the only way to change my life and achieve my dreams.

With this strong determination, I made one of the biggest decisions of my life: I traveled to China to pursue higher education. Leaving my family and my home country was not easy. It was a journey full of challenges, cultural differences, and language barriers. But I was ready to face all of these challenges because I believed in myself. When I arrived in China, everything was new to me – the language, the food, the culture, and the education system. However, I adapted quickly and started my new life as an international student.

Currently, I am studying in my second semester of Computer Science at Weifang University in China. This is a very important phase of my academic life. I am learning new subjects every day and gaining practical skills that I will use in my future career. In my first semester, I was introduced to the basics of programming and computer science. I learned about algorithms, problem-solving, and the logic behind programming languages. It was a challenging but exciting experience. I realized that computer science is not just about writing code – it is about thinking creatively, solving real-world problems, and building useful applications.

In my second semester, I have learned three important programming languages: HTML, CSS, and Python. I started with HTML, which is the foundation of web development. I learned how to structure web pages, create headings, paragraphs, links, images, tables, and forms. It was amazing to see how a simple text file could become a beautiful webpage. Then, I learned CSS, which is used for styling and designing web pages. I learned about colors, fonts, layouts, flexbox, grid, and responsive design. With CSS, I can make web pages look professional and attractive. Finally, I started learning Python, which is one of the most popular programming languages in the world. Python is simple, powerful, and used in many fields such as web development, data science, artificial intelligence, and automation. I learned about variables, loops, functions, and data structures in Python. I am excited to continue learning and building projects with Python.

Looking back, I am proud of how far I have come. From a small school in Afghanistan to a university in China, my journey has been full of challenges, learning, and growth. I have learned that with hard work, patience, and faith, anything is possible. I also learned that education is the most powerful tool for changing one's life and making a positive impact on society. I am grateful to my family, my teachers, and my friends who supported me along this journey.

In the future, I want to become a skilled software developer and use my knowledge to help others. I also want to continue learning new technologies and languages. I believe that learning never ends, and I am excited for the next chapter of my life. My goal is to complete my studies, gain professional experience, and contribute to the field of computer science. I hope to inspire other students from Afghanistan and around the world to follow their dreams and never give up.

Thank you for reading my story. This is my education and learning experience – a journey of hope, hard work, and success.
